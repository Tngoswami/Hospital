Welcome to Bahrain Public Hospital FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Bahrain Public Hospital is a Specialized hospital for internal medicines.

Treatments and procedures may include lab work, radiological studies, scans, ultrasound, medication administration and physician evaluations.

All patients are first welcomed by the receptionists and then handed to the nurse. Then patients are evaluated by doctor. Patients who have higher illness (i.e. heart attack or stroke) may be treated before those patients with less severe conditions.

You can book your appointment online from our website or call to the hospital contact number for the appointment.

Upon admission to the hospital, you will remain in the Emergency Department until your inpatient room is ready. Your admitting doctor will visit you during their designated patient rounds.

For insurance support please contact the admin department for detailed process and services covered under your medical insurance. If it comes under insurance, you can upload the approved bills for insurance claim.
